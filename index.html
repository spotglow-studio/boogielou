BOOGIELOU

She's the one who turns up in an inflatable costume and leaves the dance floor absolutely ecstatic. Boogielou is a DJ who genuinely loves what she does — and that energy is impossible not to catch.

Hi! I'm boogielou (aka Charlie), a London-based DJ who plays a fusion of house music, disco/funk, UKG and bass-heavy beats — basically anything that'll get you boogie'ing! With a particular love for classic anthems with a bassy twist.
